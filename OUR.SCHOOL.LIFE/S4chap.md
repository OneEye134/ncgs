[secret-chapter-leighton-s-proposal-plans]# Secret Chapter: Leighton’s Proposal Plans

The classroom was quiet, filled with the soft scratching of pens and the occasional rustling of papers. Study period. Everyone was supposed to be working on their assignments.

Supposed to be.

Ulysses, however, had his eyes on something much more interesting—Leighton.

She was hunched over her desk, phone tilted just enough that he could see the glow of the screen reflecting off her glasses. Her fingers moved quickly, typing something before pausing as if second-guessing herself.

Suspicious.

Ulysses smirked.

> “Whatcha looking at?”

Leighton jumped slightly and immediately locked her phone.

> “Nothing.”

Now that was extra suspicious.

> “Nothing?” Ulysses echoed. “Then you won’t mind if I—”

Before she could react, he snatched the phone from her hands.

Leighton gasped.

> “Ulysses, give it back.”

He grinned, already scrolling through her search history.

> “Let’s see what you’ve been—”

Then he stopped.

His eyes widened.

> "How to propose to an annoying idiot."
> <br/>
> "Best way to propose to someone who doesn’t take anything seriously."
> <br/>
> "What happens if you propose and they just laugh at you."
> <br/>
> "Can you take back a proposal if it goes horribly wrong."
> <br/>
> "Ways to make sure someone doesn’t think you’re joking when you propose."
> <br/>
> "How to give a ring without looking like a total fool."
> <br/>
> "Signs that someone won’t reject your proposal immediately."

Ulysses blinked. Once. Twice.

Then a slow smirk spread across his face.

> “Leighton…” He turned to her, voice dangerously teasing.

Leighton was frozen, her face an alarming shade of red.

> “... Give it back.”

But Ulysses was already reading more.

> "What if the person you like is the most annoying person in the world but you like them anyway."

Ulysses let out a sharp laugh.

> “Leighton.”

She lunged for the phone, but he dodged effortlessly, lifting it above his head.

> “Wait, wait, let me process this,” he said, grinning. “So you—”

Leighton punched him in the arm.

> “Forget. Everything.”
> “Oh, no no no, this is way too good.” He scrolled back up. “‘How to propose to an annoying idiot’? I knew you secretly liked me.”

Leighton groaned, face buried in her hands.

Ulysses leaned closer, lowering his voice mockingly.

> “Soooo… when’s the big day?”

Leighton, still red, muttered,

> “I hate you.”
> “No, no, you love me. Clearly.”

Leighton finally looked up, glaring at him.

> “I will actually kill you.”

Ulysses only laughed.

> “Not before you propose, you won’t.”
