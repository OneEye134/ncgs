Secret Chapter: The Ultimate Disaster

Ulysses had done a lot of things to annoy Leighton before.

But this? This was on a whole other level.

The Fatal Mistake

It all started when Ulysses, out of pure mischief, snatched Leighton’s phone while they were hanging out in the classroom after school.

Leighton was distracted, quietly scribbling in her notebook, so she didn’t notice right away.

Ulysses grinned.

“Let’s see what she’s been up to…”

He wasn’t expecting much—maybe some boring messages, a few embarrassing search histories.

But then he saw it.

A game.

Not just any game.

It was an MMORPG she had been playing for five years straight.

The moment he clicked on it, a pop-up appeared:

“Are you sure you want to delete this game?”

Ulysses meant to press "Cancel."

He really did.

But his finger slipped.

And in that moment—

Everything. Was. Gone.

The Moment of Realization

A cold chill ran down his spine.

No. No, no, no, no.

He quickly tried to undo it.
Checked the app store, searched for a recovery option—nothing.

It was gone.

Ulysses froze.

Then, very, very slowly… he turned his head.

Leighton was still writing in her notebook.

She hadn’t noticed.

Maybe she won’t find out until later, Ulysses thought.
Maybe I can run away before then.

Then—

“…Where’s my phone?”

Ulysses flinched.

Leighton turned. She saw the phone in his hands.

Her eyes narrowed.

“…What are you doing?”

“…Nothing.”

Leighton reached for the phone. Ulysses pulled it away.

A beat of silence.

Then—

“…Ulysses.”

The way she said his name sent a shiver down his spine.

She grabbed the phone, checked it, and saw the truth.

The Chase Begins

For a moment, she didn’t move.

She just… stared at the screen.

“…You deleted it.”

Ulysses swallowed.

“Uh… It was an accident?”

The room went silent.

Then, in a voice so cold it could freeze hell itself—

“…Ulysses.”

And then she lunged.

“OH, SHOOT—!!”

Ulysses bolted out the door, Leighton hot on his heels.

“GET BACK HERE!!”

“I SAID IT WAS AN ACCIDENT!!”

Leighton grabbed a chair.

Ulysses barely ducked as it soared over his head.

The School-Wide Chase

  • Library: Ulysses dashed in, knocking over bookshelves. The librarian yelled.
  • Cafeteria: Leighton grabbed a lunch tray and threw it like a frisbee. Ulysses dodged.
  • Hallways: Ulysses slid under a janitor’s cart. Leighton jumped over it like an action movie.

“LEIGHTON, STOP!!”

“FIVE YEARS, ULYSSES. FIVE YEARS.”

Ulysses ran faster.

Final Showdown

He made it to the rooftop, out of breath, no escape left.

Leighton walked in slowly, eyes shadowed, pure rage radiating from her.

“I-I can buy you a new account?”

Silence.

Leighton cracked her knuckles.

Ulysses gulped.

Screams echoed through the school.
